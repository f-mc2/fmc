Home

I was born and raised in Napoli where I grew up eating pizza, reading books and manga, playing guitar, and, most importantly, wondering about abstractness during my BSc, MSc, and PhD at the Università degli Studi di Napoli Federico II under the supervision of G. Marmo.

Academic trajectory

  • July 2021 - Present: Investigador distinguido in the Department of Mathematics at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M), in the Q-Math research group.
  • July 2018 - June 2021: Postdoctoral researcher in J. Jost’s group at the Max Planck Institute for the Mathematics in the Sciences (MIS-MPG).

Funding, projects et similia

  • January 2025 - Young principal researcher of the project “QUITEMAD”, 01/2025 -12/2027.
  • May 2022 - Secondary proposer of the COST project “CaLISTA”, 10/2022 - 10/2026.
  • November 2021 - Principal researcher of the project “Classical and Quantum Information Theory and Functional Analysis: Foundations and applications“, 11/2021 - 12/2024.
  • September 2021 - Member of the research team of the project “GRUPOIDES, ALGEBRAS DE VON NEUMANN Y LOS FUNDAMENTOS MATEMATICOS DE LA MECANICA CUANTICA: TEORIA Y APLICACIONES“, 09/2021 - 08/2025.
  • April 2021 - Italian national scientific habilitation (ASN) in Theoretical Physics and Phyiscs of Fundamental Interactions, settore concorsuale 02/A2.
  • September 2020 - Ayuda Beatriz Galindo for a (4+1)-year position at the UC3M.

Editorial work

  • February 2021 - Present: Editor for the European Physical Journal Plus.
  • November 2020 - Present: Review Editor for Frontiers in Physics and Frontiers in Applied Mathematics and Statistics.
